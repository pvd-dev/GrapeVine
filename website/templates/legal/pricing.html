Pricing

Grapevine works with a pay as you go pricing model. No recurring payments, no subscription

Cost: €3,99 per 100 responses

You can try out Grapevine for free, as we'll cover the first 100 responses you'll receive. After these first 100 responses you can buy more responses in blocks of 100 for just €3,99.

Payment methods

Grapevine accepts iDeal payments and Credit cards. Both are handled by our payment partner bunq.

Terms and warranty

Payment is due everytime you surpass 100 new responses. Payment can done automatically from the dashboard. This counter is set back to 0 upon payment. If you have not paid you can't use Grapevine to view responses, however it will not disable the collection of new responses.

Grapevine does not refund if you only use part of your 100 responses.

Example

Laura started using grapevine and set up a survey using QR codes. Laura does not have to pay for this. After a while she receives more than 100 responses on her survey. Laura will get a notification to top up her account for €3,99. After she done so, she can receive another 100 responses before having to pay again.

How can I see how many responses I have left?

In your dashboard there will be a bar that tells you exactly how many responses you have left before you have to pay. At 80% we'll even add a banner on top of your dashboard so you can't miss it.
